Greenwood Streetscapes


  Project Description



Our neighborhood-driven project is exploring ways to build sidewalks and streetscapes to connect people and places in Greenwood.


Our vision is a Greenwood community that is walkable, attractive, connected and sustainable. This might include sidewalks, trees, planted buffers and natural drainage.


  In 2010 we plan to:

  • Engage Greenwood residents to find out what they would like to see on their street.

  • Map every Greenwood block north of 85th that has an incomplete street.

  • Develop a neighborhood-wide streetscape plan.

  • Research tools to fund and coordinate streetscape projects.

  • Create a user-friendly web-based guide so people can create streetscape solutions for their own blocks.     

  • Identify a few pilot blocks that are interested in adding sidewalks and develop schematic designs for them.



The project is funded by neighborhood donations and the City of Seattle Neighborhood Matching Grant Program.
